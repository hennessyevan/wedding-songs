The suggestion tags are meant to help you find songs for each part of the mass/service.


These songs are ones that we've played at weddings in the past that we think you'll like. It should open a page where you can listen to it on your platform of choice.

While we are open to other selections, the instruction of the Catholic Church required that a song needs to be able to be considered appropriate as a hymn to be played in a liturgical setting. This usually means that the song must reference or be based on scripture, the liturgy, the Sacraments, etc.

We often play other hymns at mass on Sunday that are beautiful as well, any of those hymns can be chosen.
