Nativity Band Wedding Hymns

Below you will find a list of songs that we played at weddings in the past.


You'll notice tags on each song that are suggestions of when to include them in the wedding service or mass. While we think these are helpful, feel free to use these hymns at any point in the mass/service.


While we are open to other hymn suggestions, the instruction of the Catholic Church required that a song needs to be able to be considered appropriate as a hymn to be played at a mass. This usually means that the song references or is based on scripture, the liturgy, the Sacraments, etc.


This includes any songs that you hear at one of our masses, worship songs, traditional hymns, songs that speak directly about the liturgy of the Sacrament of Marriage or songs that are based on scripture, etc.
